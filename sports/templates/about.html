About

  • Sports Resources Booking Application is the management of all the resources of an organization.

  • The Admin can add and delete the resources when required.

  • Available resources are requested to issue by the users and the admin can accept or reject the booking request based on the blocked users list.

  • When the users do not return the resources or lose the item then they are blocked until the user pays the fine.

  • The fines are collected manually by the admin and the users are unblocked.
